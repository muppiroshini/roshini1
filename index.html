atm system

The main aim of this project to provide secure banking system, by taking fingerprints as authorized identity at ATM/banks. The purpose of the project is to provide a secured and reliable environment to the customers for their banking transactions by providing a unique identity to every user using the FINGER PRINT identification technology. Identification and verification of a person today is a common thing; which may include door-lock system, safe box and vehicle control or even at accessing bank accounts via ATM, etc which is necessary for securing personal information.
